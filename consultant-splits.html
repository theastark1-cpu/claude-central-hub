How this is calculated: The TPA performance fee per investor is the GP pool. We attribute each investor's perf fee to the consultant who raised them (per IDS), then split the GP pool: Fund Mgmt 59.5%, Consultant 39%, Raj/Nairne/Alec(GP) 0.5% each. TruQuant's 18% is taken upstream and is not on Armada Prime's books. Costs are weighted by each party's share of the GP pool — if you earn 30% of GP this month, you pay 30% of the costs.
